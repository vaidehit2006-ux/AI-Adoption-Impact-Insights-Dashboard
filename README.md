# AI Adoption & Impact Insights Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes AI adoption trends across industries using real-time survey responses collected through Google Forms. The responses are connected to Google Sheets and visualized in Power BI, enabling live data refresh and interactive insights.

## Problem Statement

As AI adoption grows rapidly, understanding how people use AI, the benefits they experience, and the challenges they face is crucial. This dashboard provides a data-driven view of AI adoption and impact across different sectors.

## Key Insights

- 94% AI adoption rate
- 89% expect future benefits from AI
- Average helpfulness rating: 4/5
- Education & IT lead adoption
- ChatGPT is the most used AI tool
- Information overload and privacy concerns are major challenges
